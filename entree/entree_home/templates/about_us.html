About Us


In the changing world each of us desire and aspire to contribute towards sustainable growth. Among many growth blockers, one of the biggest contributor is unemployment.

ITEntréè is here to make a small change for a bigger IMPACT. We are here to connect the dots between the employment providers and the employment seekers. We all know the struggle involved to get into a first job. Truth is 60% of 1.5M graduates do not get a chance, 20% of them do not get into a suitable job and let's not talk about how many find their dream job.

ITEntréè believe in individual growth is the key to collective success. We spark the minds of young graduates to ignite creativity,logical and analytical thinking which help finding their dream job.

Many recruiters step back to hire young graduates because of many challenging factors but young graduates need not worry. We at ITEntréè accept the challenge.

We are a team of dedicated and quality people who share the same enthusiasm and dream of supporting, nurturing and helping young graduates until they find their desired job.

Our mission is to reduce the void between the graduates and the recruiters.
